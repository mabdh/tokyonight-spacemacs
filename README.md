# tokyonight-spacemacs

Tokyonight Color Scheme for Spacemacs inspired by [folke/tokyonight.nvim](https://github.com/folke/tokyonight.nvim)

```
(custom-set-variables
 '(spacemacs-theme-custom-colors
   '((base . "#c0caf5")
     (border . "#3d59a1")
     (bg1 . "#1a1b26")
     (cblk . "#1abc9c")
     (cblk-bg . "#1a1b26")
     (cblk-ln-bg . "#3346c7")
     (cursor . "#a9b1d6")
     (const . "#ff9e64")
     (comment  . "#565f89")
     (comment-bg . "#1a1b26")
     (err . "#db4b4b")
     (func . "#7aa2f7")
     (head1 . "#bb9af7")
     (head2 . "#7aa2f7")
     (head3-bg . "#ffffff")
     (highlight . "#3b4261")
     (lnum . "#3b4261")
     (keyword . "#bb9af7")
     (mat . "#ff9e64")
     (meta . "#86dc2f")
     (str . "#9ece6a")
     (suc . "#73daca")
     (type . "#f1fa8c")
     (var . "#bb9af7")
     (war . "#e0af68"))))
```


## How To Install

Add above expression to `dotspacemacs/user-init`.
